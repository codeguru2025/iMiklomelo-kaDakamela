PROJECT OVERVIEW

Build a production-ready, mobile-first, high-scalability web application for Imiklomelo Ka Dakamela, the official Chief Dakamela Achievers Awards & Cultural Gathering.

The platform must handle hundreds of thousands of users, including international guests, under intermittent connectivity, while enforcing secure identity, payments, access control, and cultural authority.

This is a LIVE SYSTEM (not a demo).

🚫 CULTURAL & FUNCTIONAL NON-NEGOTIABLES

❌ No public nominations

❌ No award categories

❌ No voting

❌ No popularity scoring

Awards are selected and conferred solely by the Chief.
The platform only documents official outcomes and history.
