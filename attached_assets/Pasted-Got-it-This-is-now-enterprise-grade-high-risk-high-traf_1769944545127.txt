# 🔱 MEGA-PROMPT

## **Imiklomelo Ka Dakamela – Official Event, Camping, Services & Heritage Platform**

---

## 1. System Objective (Locked)

Build a **mission-critical, mobile-first, high-availability web application** for **Imiklomelo Ka Dakamela**, the official **Chief Dakamela Achievers Awards & Cultural Gathering**.

The platform must support **hundreds of thousands of users**, including **international attendees**, under **unstable network conditions**, while **protecting cultural authority and data integrity**.

The system serves as:

* The **official booking & reservation platform**
* The **services selection & deposit payment system**
* The **exhibitor & sponsor onboarding system**
* The **historical archive of past events and awardees**
* The **cultural record of Imiklomelo Ka Dakamela**

⚠️ **Governance Rule (ABSOLUTE):**
There are **NO public nominations, NO award categories, NO voting**.
All awards are **privately decided and conferred by the Chief**.
The platform **only displays past and official outcomes**.

---

## 2. Core User Roles

### A. Public (No Login)

* View upcoming event details
* View **past editions of Imiklomelo Ka Dakamela**
* View:

  * Past awardees
  * Photos
  * Awards conferred
* View confirmed sponsors (Kingdom Blue as **Main Sponsor**)
* View approved exhibitors
* View accommodation & camping options (availability only)

---

### B. Attendees (Local & Global)

#### Mandatory Capabilities

* Register attendance
* Select attendance type:

  * Standard
  * VIP
  * Delegation / Cultural Guest
* Declare:

  * Country & city of origin
  * Arrival & departure dates

#### Camping & Accommodation Booking (Critical)

* Select **camp type**
* Select **optional services during booking**, such as:

  * Tent
  * Bedding
  * Meals
  * Power access
  * Security locker
  * Transport (if offered)
* Services are **capacity-based and time-bound**

#### Payments

* User must **pay a DEPOSIT** to secure their camping space
* No deposit = **NO reservation**
* Deposit status must be:

  * Pending
  * Paid
  * Expired
  * Refunded (admin only)
* Reservation auto-expires if deposit not paid within a defined window

---

### C. Exhibitors & Sponsors (Companies)

* Company registration
* Select role:

  * Exhibitor
  * Sponsor
  * Both
* Exhibition categories:

  * Art
  * Fashion
  * Food
  * Cultural Crafts
  * Services
* Sponsorship tiers
* Upload branding assets
* Await admin approval

⚠️ **Kingdom Blue**

* Hard-coded as **Primary Sponsor**
* Always displayed first
* Cannot be removed via UI

---

### D. Admin (Chief’s Committee)

* Manage:

  * Accommodation inventory
  * Service capacity
  * Deposits & confirmations
* Approve exhibitors & sponsors
* Publish official announcements
* Manage **past event archive**
* Upload:

  * Awardee names
  * Photos
  * Awards conferred
* Export operational reports

⚠️ No admin interfaces for nominations or voting.

---

## 3. Past Events & Cultural Archive (Mandatory)

The system must include a **Historical Archive Module**:

### Each Past Event Includes:

* Year / Edition
* Event summary
* List of awardees
* Awards conferred
* Image gallery
* Optional video embeds
* Sponsors of that year

This module is **read-only for the public** and **admin-managed only**.

This is **non-optional** and must be optimized for content delivery at scale.

  * Payments
